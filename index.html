Questions

Frequently Answered

Discover details about our procedures, technology, and clinical standards.

LuxeSmile offers a highly personalized clinical journey. Unlike standard practices that prioritize volume, we book one guest at a time in our luxury penthouse suite, ensuring zero wait times and full privacy. Furthermore, our clinical specialists combine high-magnification dental microscopy with premium aesthetic skin-matching algorithms for teeth restoration.
Patient comfort is at the core of our brand. We offer premium amenities including noise-canceling headsets with custom soundscapes, aromatherapy diffusion, heated ergonomic dental chairs, and optional mild conscious sedation (nitrous oxide or oral conscious sedation) administered by certified specialists.
Most smile transformations are completed in just two visits. During the first visit, we take digital intraoral scans and prepare your teeth (if necessary) to seat temporary veneers. On the second visit, usually 10-14 days later, your custom, hand-layered porcelain veneers are permanently bonded.
LuxeSmile works as an out-of-network clinic to guarantee we never compromise on treatment design or dental material choices. Our concierge team manages all paperwork, submissions, and filing directly with your PPO provider for reimbursement. We also offer bespoke interest-free in-house installments for smile design services.
