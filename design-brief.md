# Design Brief: LuxeSmile Clinic

## Design Read
An ultra-luxury boutique dental wellness experience in Hyderabad, combining surgical precision with spa-like serenity, designed for elite clients who view dental health as an art.

## Concept Spine
"The Artistry of a Smile" – tracing the journey from raw clinical precision to the final aesthetic masterpiece.

## Locked Palette
- Ground: `#0D0C0B` (Onyx Black)
- Surface: `#171512` (Dark Champagne)
- Ink: `#F5EFE3` (Ivory White)
- Muted: `#A79E8E` (Warm Platinum)
- Accent (only one): `#C8A96A` (Champagne Gold)

*Defense*: The palette mirrors the physical environment of the clinic – dark brushed metal walls, warm amber lighting, ivory marble, and gold trim.

## Locked Type
- Display: **Cabinet Grotesk** (500/700/800) – architectural, modern, confident.
- Body: **Inter Tight** (400) – clinical, crisp, highly legible.

## Hero Technique
**Option A: Aperture Mask Reveal**. 
*Defense*: The transition from a small, focused circular window into a full-bleed panoramic film view mimics the clinical focus of a microscope opening up to reveal a beautiful, expansive smile.

## Section Plan
1. **Hero Reveal**: Pinned film aperture mask expansion.
2. **Proof Strip**: Metric facts with hairline borders (Hyderabad location, satisfy audit).
3. **Space Chapter**: Sticky film scroll through the luxury Gachibowli clinic.
4. **Journey**: Numbered steps 01-04 of the treatment journey.
5. **Offerings**: Interactive click-to-expand list of 4 core treatments.
6. **Technology**: Split layout showing microscopic clinical precision.
7. **Protocol Band**: statement + middot list detailing single-guest privacy.
8. **FAQ Accordion**: 5 clean, transparent Q&As.
9. **Quote Statement**: Oversized patient quote with citation.
10. **Visit Finale**: Full-bleed background video with float booking card (`id="visit"`).

## CTA Inventory
- Primary: "Reserve Consultation" -> opens scheduler/scrolls to booking form.
- Secondary: "Explore Treatments" -> scrolls to offerings.
- Footer Link: "concierge@luxesmile.com" -> email deep link.
